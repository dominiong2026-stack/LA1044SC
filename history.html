Historical note

A San Fernando Lodge with Scottish Constitution continuity.

Established by charter from the Grand Lodge of Scotland on 7 May 1908, amended on 6 November 1919, and erected and consecrated in San Fernando in 1920.

Mount Horeb

Meeting place

The Temple, Mount Horeb, Ruth Avenue, Les Efforts West, San Fernando, Trinidad and Tobago.

Regular meetings

Meeting pattern

Regular Meetings are held on the first Thursday of February, March, April, May, June, July, September, October, and November. Installation is held on the second Saturday of December.

1944 legal milestone

Incorporated trustees

Ordinance No. 37 of 1944 incorporated the trustees of Lodge Alexandra of Freemasons, in Trinidad. Named trustees included Donald Daniel Bissessar, Right Worshipful Master; Garvin Montagu Scott, Worshipful Senior Warden; and Alexander Roland Bynoe, Worshipful Junior Warden.

Past Masters

Continuity of leadership

The Lodge records a long line of Past Masters from Dr. Phillip T. Vilain in 1919 through Bro. Shastri V.C. Parsad in 2025. The list includes distinguished Brethren such as Dr. William H. Nightingale, Thomas L. Dunsire, Justice Garvin M. Scott, Barry P. Hubah, Adrian C. Saunders, Graeme C. Gillezeau, Wilfred Jackson, Colin Jones, and Shastri V.C. Parsad.
