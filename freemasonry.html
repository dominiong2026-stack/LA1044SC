Freemasonry

A disciplined fraternity for moral reflection, fellowship, charity, and service.

Freemasonry uses allegory, symbolism, ceremony, and fellowship to encourage self-improvement, integrity, benevolence, and duty to others.

Brotherly Love

Respect, civility, fraternity, and mutual support among Brethren.

Relief

Charitable duty, care, and practical concern for others.

Truth

Integrity, moral seriousness, reflection, and personal discipline.

Masonic charity

The Lodge recognises charity as a practical expression of Masonic duty. TTMPS and TTMCO are identified as part of the local charitable landscape; full descriptions can be added once final approved wording is supplied.
