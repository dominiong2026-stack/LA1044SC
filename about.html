About the Lodge

Formal Lodge governance with a contemporary public presence.

Lodge Alexandra No. 1044 S.C. is a Scottish Constitution Lodge meeting at Mount Horeb, Ruth Avenue, Les Efforts West, San Fernando. Established by charter from the Grand Lodge of Scotland on 7 May 1908 and amended on 6 November 1919.
